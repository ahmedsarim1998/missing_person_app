About locAIte

Bringing Families Back Together with AI

locAIte is an AI-powered missing person recovery platform built at IQRA University. We combine facial recognition, real-time CCTV analysis, and community reporting to detect and locate missing individuals — turning a slow, manual search into a fast, intelligent process that helps reunite families sooner.
