Facebook Sightings

Paste a Facebook post to auto-extract a last-seen location and update the matching case. Every processed match is logged below (and is reversible).

Recommended format (required fields in bold):
Name: <full name of an active case>
Age: <optional>
Last seen: <area, city>
Contact: <optional phone>
Only Name and Last seen are required. Free-form posts (“…was last seen near Saddar”) also work.
The post just needs an active case's name and where they were last seen.
Pulls recent posts from your configured Facebook group (needs FB_GROUP + FB_COOKIES). Can take up to a minute.
Generates sample posts from active cases (for testing the flow).
